THIS CAUSE came before the Board of Medicine (Board)
pursuant to section 120.57(3), Florida Statutes, on October 5,
1990, in Miami, Florida, for the purpose of considering
Respondent's Voluntary Relinquishment of License (attached hereto
as Exhibit A). This voluntary relinquishment was in response to
an Administrative Complaint (attached hereto as Exhibit B)
alleging Respondent's failure to comply with a prior Final Order
of this Board which was filed on or about February 23, 1989
(attached hereto as Exhibit C). Petitioner was represented at
the hearing by Stephanie A. Daniel, Chief Medical Attorney;
Respondent was given notice but was not present.

Upon consideration of the Voluntary Relinquishment of
License, the documents and pleadings of record, and being

otherwise fully advised in the premises,

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED AND ADJUDGED:

That Respondent's Voluntary Relinquishment of License with
the provision that Respondent agrees never again to apply-for
licensure as a physician in the State of Florida is hereby
ACCEPTED.

This Order takes effect upon filing with the Clerk of the
Department of Professional Regulation.

COMES NOW, the Petitioner, Department of Professional
Regulation, hereinafter referred to as “Petitioner," and files
this Administrative Complaint before the Board of Medicine
against Donald F. Bosker, M.D., hereinafter referred to as
”Respondent," and alleges:

1. Petitioner is the state agency charged with regulating
the practice of medicine pursuant to Section 20.30, Florida
Statutes, Chapter 455, Flarida Statutes, and Chapteh 458, Florida
Statutes.

2. Respondent is and has been at all times material
hereto a licensed physician in the State of Florida, having been
issued license number ME 0022645. Respondent's last known
address is 1198 North Belsay Road, Flint, Michigan 48509-1600.

3. On or about February 23, 1989, pursuant to the Final
Order in Case Number 0099394, Respondent was required to pay an

Administrative fine of 3500 within thirty (30) daiiéa'.

4. On or about November 22, 1989, and on or about
December 28, 1989, Subject was notified by mail that he was
required to pay this fine. Respondent has failed to pay this
fine.

5. Respondent violated a lawful order of the Board
previously entered in a disciplinary hearing in that Respondent
has failed to pay an administratiave fine pursuant to Case Number
0099394.

6. Based on the preceding allegations Respondent violated
Section 458.33l(l)(x), Flcrida Statutes, by violating any
provision of this chapter, a rule of the board or department, or
a lawful order of the board of department previously enteted in a
disciplinary hearing or failing to comply with a lawfully issued
subpoena of the department.

WHEREFORE, Petitioner respectfully requests the Board of
Medicine enter an Order imposing one or more of the following
penalties: revocation or suspension of the Respondent's license,
restriction of the Respondent's practice, imposition of an
administrative fine, issuance of a reprimand, placement of the

Respondent on probation, and/or any other relief that the Board

deems appropriate.
