THIS CAUSE came before the Board of Medicine (Board)
pursuant to Section 120.57(2), Florida Statutes, on April 12,
1991, in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, for consideration of the
Administrative Complaint (attached hereto as Exhibit A) in the
above—styled cause. At the hearing, Petitioner was represented
by Larry G.McPherson, Jr., Attorney at Law; Respondent was duly
notified of the hearing and was not present. The facts are not
in dispute.

Upon consideration, it is ORDERED:

1. The allegations of fact set forth in the Administrative
Complaint are approved and adopted and incorporated herein by
reference as the findings of fact by the Board, with the
exception that Paragraph 4 is corrected to reflect the ending
date of May 6, 1979.

2. The conclusions of law alleged and set forth in the
Administrative Complaint are approved and adopted and

incorporated herein by reference as the conclusions of law by the

Board.

3. The violations set forth warrant disciplinary action by

the Board. THEREFORE,
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED AND ADJUDGED:

Respondent's license to practice medicine is REPRIMANDED.

Department of Professional Regulation.
